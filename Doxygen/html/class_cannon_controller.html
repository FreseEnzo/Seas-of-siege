Detailed Description

Controls the behavior of a cannon, including aiming, targeting, and firing cannonballs.

This class manages the targeting of enemies within a certain range, automatically aiming at the closest enemy, and firing cannonballs at a specified rate. It handles the rotation of the cannon to align with the target and instantiates cannonballs with a set force.
